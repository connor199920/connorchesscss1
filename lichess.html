Lichess

Lichess is a free, open-source, and ad-free chess platform that has quickly become one of the most popular alternatives to other chess websites. With over 150,000 daily active users, Lichess is built by the chess community for the chess community. Unlike many other chess sites, Lichess is completely free with no paywalls or subscriptions required. It’s an excellent resource for both casual players and competitive enthusiasts who want to enjoy chess without interruptions. Lichess was the website where I learned to play chess, and I really benefited from the free analysis and the great structure of the website. You can choose to become a Lichess patron if you'd like to give back to the website, although you will get all the same perks either way.

The platform offers a range of features such as real-time play, puzzles, and daily chess challenges. Users can also participate in tournaments, analyze their games using powerful engine analysis tools, or study chess openings and endgames. Lichess has a vibrant community where players can engage in discussions, contribute to its open-source development, and watch live streams of chess events. It also supports advanced tools like custom variants and allows players to study famous games or learn from the vast database of chess puzzles.

Lichess is committed to keeping chess accessible to everyone, regardless of location or skill level. The site’s clean, intuitive interface and strong commitment to free, open-source software have made it a standout in the online chess world.
