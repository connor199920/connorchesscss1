Chessable

Chessable is a premier online chess learning platform, specializing in chess openings, tactics, and strategy, with a focus on repetition and long-term memory retention. Founded by chess enthusiasts, the platform has become a go-to source for players looking to improve their chess skills through structured learning. Chessable’s innovative “MoveTrainer” technology helps players memorize chess openings, sequences, and patterns through spaced repetition, ensuring that users retain what they learn over time.
